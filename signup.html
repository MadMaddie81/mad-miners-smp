How Do I Join
MadMiners SMP?

To join the MadMiners SMP server you will first have to join our Discord server.

Since we are an international server and we all live in different time zones and play on different hours, Discord is our main way of comunicating with each other.

Note that because of our wide spread members and the fact that we all are adults with jobs and real life obligations, most of the time you play on the server you will probably be by yourself.

Think of it as playing on a solo world, but you have awesome neighbors, and you have access to the world from any device at any time.

You can find the actual application form on Discord.
